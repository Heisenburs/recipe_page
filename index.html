Jig's Milkshake Recipe

Milkshakes are not supposed to be healthy. This is a proper milkshake recipe. If you are not breathing heavily after your first sip. Start again! You didn't do it correctly.

Ingredients

  • Ice Cream( of your choice! )
  • Half & Half
  • Splash of Vanilla Extract
  • Maraschino Cherries
  • Whipped cream

Method

  1. Add ice cream to the blender.
  2. Add half & half to blender.
  3. Make sure to add just a splash of vanilla extract.
  4. Blend on medium until desired consistency.
  5. Pour into glass and garnish with whipped cream, Maraschino cherries, and any other toppings you like.
  6. Enjoy!
